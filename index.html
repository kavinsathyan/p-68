The School Mission

The School imparts knowledge without any discrimination of caste, creed or community. The School strives to inculcate values and ethics into the minds of students, so as to enable them to build a noble society. The School moulds the students with utmost Patriotism, enabling them to value the Nation the most. The students are imbibed with the quality to respect the feelings of all others, especially the poor, down-trodden and the needy.
